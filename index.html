John Ronald Reuel (J.R.R.) Tolkien was an English scholar, professor and author. Tolkien is most well known for his work creating the Middle Earth universe via The Hobbit and the Lord of the Rings triliogy. His work is still recognized today as some of the most complex, comprehensive and immersive in history. A complete biography can be found on the Tolkien Society's Site.
